Know more

  • How to Join?

    EduTools is open to everyone, and there's no need to sign up or create an account. Simply visit our website and start using the tools immediately.

  • Is it Paid or Free?

    EduTools is completely free to use. We believe in providing valuable resources without any cost, making our tools accessible to everyone who needs them.

  • What are the benefits?

    By using Edu Tools Hub, you gain access to a variety of student-focused tools all in one place, including a sorting visualizer, advanced calculator, to-do list, and fee calculator. These tools are designed to boost productivity, support learning, and help students stay organized and efficient.

  • What types of resources are provided?

    Edu Tools Hub offers a range of practical tools specially built for students, including a Sorting Visualizer, Advanced Calculator, To-Do List, and College Fee Calculator.

  • How can I contribute to community?

    While Edu Tools Hub does not currently have a formal community contribution system, you can support our project by sharing it with classmates and friends who may find it useful.

  • Can I network with other members?

    At the moment, Edu Tools Hub does not include direct networking features. However, we encourage users to share their feedback and experiences on social media and our mail. Stay tuned for future updates that may include community-based features!
